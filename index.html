About Me

I am a dual-major in International Studies and Political Science at the University of Idaho, focusing on the intersection of international relations, sociology, and political ideologies.

My goal is to understand how the world works—both through human systems and digital ones. Beyond my academic studies, I am deeply interested in intelligence analysis, languages, and penetration testing, using my technical background in Python and hardware to explore the vulnerabilities in modern systems.

This portfolio serves as a documentation of my work, bridging the gap between rigorous political analysis and cybersecurity innovation.
